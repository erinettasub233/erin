I question what’s assumed, analyze what works and break what doesn’t.

I’m Manu Medina, a product designer with a strong academic background, and a mindset that questions before builds. I see design as a structured and intentional process that focuses on solving real problems.

I approach each project as a way to shape meaningful, functional and well-crafted experiences. I enjoy working in teams where communication is direct, ideas are valued, and the goal is to build solutions that truly work. I’m especially drawn to environments where design meets business and creates real impact.

Despite having just finished my design degree and graduating with eight honors, including this distinction in my final thesis, I have already worked in creative agencies contributing to projects for brands like Prada, Tiffany & Co., Forbes or Aliexpress while working with creative agencies.
